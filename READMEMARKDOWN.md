# Heading level 1
> Don't put tabs or spaces in front of your paragraphs.

``
def func():
    while True:
        print("Karol jest glupi")
``

Don't put tabs or **spaces** in front *of* your *paragraphs*.

Keep lines ***left-aligned*** like ~~this~~.

1. First item
2. Second item
3. Third item
    1. Indented item
    2. Indented item
4. Fourth item 

- First item
- Second item
- Third item
    - Indented item
    - Indented item
- Fourth item
